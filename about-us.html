About Us

Things you may want to know about Careerer & more.

Who is behind Careerer?

Not a billion dollar company, we're just students! Careerer was first designed by university students as a school project, but now it's getting real!

Who funds Careerer?

Careerer started as an idea and was announced at Kickstarter. With donations from people who believed in us, we made enough funding to make Careerer posibble.

How does Careerer help?

Careerer simply tracks your career. It searchs jobs for you, helps you with your CV and does much more. For more information on it's features, visit our Explore Features page.

Our Values

The principles that shape everything we do are: Innovation, Accessibility, Trust, Growth.
